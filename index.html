Welcome to SonicParp!

The website where you can share your drawings of Sonic and friends playing tubas and sousaphones!

The verb "parp" means "to honk loudly, like a horn". This word is a very fitting name for my website because it showcases drawings of the Sonic gang honking loudly on tubas and sousaphones, which are large brass instruments or "contrabass horns".

These pictures will be placed in three different categories, or "Parp Levels":

  • Parp Level 1, which features the character playing the tuba or sousaphone with regular-sized puffed-out cheeks and open eyes.
  • Parp Level 2, which features the character playing the tuba or sousaphone with regular-sized puffed-out cheeks and closed eyes.
  • Parp Level 3, which features the character playing the tuba or sousaphone with extra-large puffed-out cheeks, closed eyes, and a red, blue, or purple face, with zigzag lines coming out of the instrument's bell.

If you want to submit your pictures of Sonic and his mates parping, email them to me at beanslyons@gmail.com or 726197@sylvaniastudents.org. I will be submitting my own drawings as well.
